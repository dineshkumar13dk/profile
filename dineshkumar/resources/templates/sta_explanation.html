SMART TRAVELLERS APP - Explanation

Abstraction

The SMART TRAVELLERS APP (STA) aims to revolutionize the travel experience by providing users with an integrated platform to plan, book, and manage trips. It leverages advanced technologies to deliver personalized travel recommendations.

Introduction

This project was developed using Flutter, Dart, Firebase, and Google Cloud. The STA app simplifies travel logistics and provides real-time updates, helping travelers navigate challenges seamlessly.

Objectives

  • To provide a user-friendly interface for managing travel plans.
  • To enable real-time updates for bookings and transportation.
  • To integrate AI-based travel recommendations for users.
