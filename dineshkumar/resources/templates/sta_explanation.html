SMART TRAVELLERS APP - Explanation

Back to Projects

Abstraction

The SMART TRAVELLERS APP (STA) is designed to simplify the travel experience by providing users with a platform for planning, booking, and managing their trips. It utilizes modern technology for personalized recommendations and real-time updates.

Introduction

The STA app is built using Flutter, Dart, Firebase, and Google Cloud, offering a seamless travel experience. With an intuitive interface, it helps users with trip planning, real-time updates, and hassle-free travel management.

Objectives

  • To provide a user-friendly interface for planning and managing trips.
  • To offer real-time notifications for bookings and travel updates.
  • To integrate AI-driven travel suggestions based on user preferences.
